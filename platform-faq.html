3. Data, Privacy and Authorization

How is behavioral data handled?

The platform processes necessary data only under user authorization. Processing principles include de-identification, anonymization, desensitization and aggregation. Personal identity information is not disclosed externally.

What can data be used for?

Data may support AI model training, behavioral science research, product optimization, control-system research and data product development. It is not used for real financial execution.

Will data be provided to third parties?

If data collaboration exists, it is limited to anonymized, compliant model data or research data without personal identity information. The platform does not sell personal privacy information.

Why independent cloud environments?

Simulation environments and long-term behavioral records require consistency. Cloud environments improve stability, reduce interruption risk, maintain environmental consistency, avoid record contamination and support long-running training.

4. Control System and Platform Rules

What is the control circuit-breaker system?

It is a simulation training support tool. It may include maximum drawdown limits, abnormal behavior reminders, cooldown periods and forced restriction mechanisms. It applies only to simulation environments and is not investment advice.

Serious violations

Serious violations include real-trading solicitation, guaranteed-return promotion, external leveraged-platform referral, data cheating, system tampering, confidential information leakage, multi-account collusion, impersonation and illegal behavior. The platform may warn, restrict, suspend or terminate authorization.

5. Risk and Responsibility Boundary

If users privately use tools for real trading, is the platform responsible?

No. Platform products are authorized only for simulation, backtesting, behavioral training and scientific research. Any risk arising from a user's introduction of tools into real financial scenarios is borne by the user.

Does the platform promise improvement?

No. The platform does not guarantee high levels, data incentives, career improvement, earning ability or commercial returns. Results depend on user behavior and project rules.

Final Compliance Statement

BK-GTA always belongs to the category of simulation environment, data engineering, SaaS technology services and business outsourcing. It does not conduct financial business, touch real funds or provide investment advice.

All fees correspond to software, computing resources and technical services. All data processing follows privacy protection and authorization principles. Any interpretation that presents BK-GTA as a real trading platform, investment project, guaranteed-return plan or recruitment project does not represent the official position of the platform.
