Privacy Policy

Version v1.0 · Official domain: ziyingke.com

This policy explains how BK-GTA, known in Chinese as 自营客, may collect, use and protect data related to simulation environments, cloud computers, behavioral analytics and technical services.

1. Data We May Collect

  • Account data, such as username, email, login credentials and subscription status.
  • Device and security data, such as IP address, browser, operating system, login logs and risk signals.
  • Cloud computer and simulation data, such as session status, environment logs, task records and technical errors.
  • Behavioral data, such as decision paths, execution deviations, response timing, stability metrics and training reports.

2. How Data Is Used

Data may be used to provide platform access, maintain cloud computers, operate simulation systems, generate behavioral analytics, improve training paths, support users, protect system safety and produce anonymized research insights.

3. Simulation Data Boundary

Simulation data is not used for real-money execution, investment advice, return promises, broker onboarding, copy trading or financial solicitation. Research and collaboration should prioritize de-identified, anonymized and aggregated data.

4. Sharing and Service Providers

BK-GTA does not sell personal data unlawfully. Necessary data may be processed by hosting, cloud, security, analytics, payment or support providers only to the extent required for service delivery, security or legal compliance.

5. Security and Access Control

Reasonable safeguards may include HTTPS, access control, logging, permission separation, secure configuration, monitoring and audit trails. No internet system can be guaranteed absolutely secure.

6. Retention and Deletion

Data is retained only as needed for service operation, security, dispute handling, research integrity or legal obligations. Users may request correction, deletion or account closure where applicable, subject to legal, security and audit requirements.

7. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated for product, legal, security or operational reasons. Continued use after updates means you acknowledge the updated policy.
