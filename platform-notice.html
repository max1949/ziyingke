Platform Notice

Version v1.0 · Official domain: ziyingke.com

BK-GTA, known in Chinese as 自营客, is a technology platform for simulation environments, behavioral analytics and cloud computer infrastructure. Platform services are for simulation, training, research and technical support only, and do not involve real-money trading or financial execution.

1. Platform Nature

BK-GTA provides simulation decision environments, behavioral data capture and labeling, cloud computing resources, software system licensing, AI behavioral analytics and research support. BK-GTA is not a financial execution platform.

2. Non-Financial Service Statement

  • No real-money accounts, deposits, withdrawals or clearing services.
  • No execution channels for securities, futures, FX, digital assets or other financial products.
  • No investment advice, trading signals, managed trading, copy trading or asset management services.
  • No guaranteed returns, outcomes, principal protection or commercial rewards.

3. Simulation Data Notice

Accounts, indicators, tasks, levels, training records, reports and examples in the platform are for simulation or research purposes only. They do not correspond to real assets or real market outcomes.

4. Value and Use of Simulation Data

BK-GTA values simulation data because it records decision processes in controlled environments, not only result numbers. This data can support training review, behavior pattern analysis, task difficulty optimization, AI-assisted analytics, research content, system safety governance and institutional pilots.

Reasonable uses include identifying execution deviations, observing learning curves, evaluating discipline stability, comparing behavior across training scenarios, generating anonymized aggregate reports and improving platform infrastructure and product experience.

5. Data Governance Boundary

Research, reports and collaboration should prioritize de-identified, anonymized and aggregated data. Personally identifiable data should not be disclosed without authorization. Simulation data must not be packaged as return prediction, trading signals, real trading advice or proof of financial ability.

6. Community and Support Boundary

Official community and support channels only cover platform access, simulation systems, cloud computers, subscriptions, technical services and rule explanations. Investment advice, return promotion, external broker onboarding or real trading solicitation are outside BK-GTA's service scope.

7. User Acknowledgement

By continuing to access or use BK-GTA, you acknowledge this notice, the Terms and the Privacy Policy. If you are seeking investment advice, real-money trading or return opportunities, do not use this platform.
